We have openings!


Undergraduate students


If you are an undergraduate student at University of Noth Dakota and interested in exploring new avenues in computational chemistry or quantum computing, please connect with us. Research improves undergraduate experience and we are passionate at providing all the resources and training needed for you to contribute to the current body of knowledge of humanity.

Graduate students


We always have openings for graduate students!
If you are interested in developing new methods and algorithms at the exciting intersection of quantum chemistry and quantum computing, please apply to the graduate program of UND chemistry and send an email to the PI. Along with being motivated in research, ideal candidates are expected to have atleast one of the following skills: (a) General programing skills (or interest), (b) Background in physical chemistry, (c) Background in quantum computing algorithms or noise-mitigation techniques (or interest), (d) Computational research experience with materials or molecules.
