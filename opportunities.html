Opportunities

Undergraduate, graduate, and postdoctoral pathways for joining the Asthana Group.

Postdoctoral research associate

We may have an opening soon. Please connect if you are interested.

Undergraduate students

If you are an undergraduate student in any STEM department at the University of North Dakota and interested in exploring computational chemistry or quantum computing, please connect with us.

Graduate students

We have openings for 1-2 graduate students. If you are interested in developing new methods and algorithms at the intersection of quantum chemistry, quantum computing, and machine learning, please apply.

Students can expect to build skills in quantum algorithms, computational chemistry, and scientific software engineering in a collaborative and supportive atmosphere. UND offers a competitive stipend and regular opportunities to attend leading conferences.

We are hiring across multiple roles. Please connect with the PI to discuss collaborations or open positions.
