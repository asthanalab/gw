🎉 March 2026: DOE ARPA-E QC3 award for Asthana Group. We are excited to be part of the 2026-2029 DOE ARPA-E QC3 project, "Quantum-Classical Ab Initio Co-Simulation of Unconventional Superconductors" (QASUS), in collaboration with Infleqtion and NREL. We will serve as a quantum chemistry and quantum algorithms bridge between NREL's expertise in material simulation and Infleqtion's advanced quantum devices. This is a $3.9 million project, with $600,000 supporting the Asthana Group. ARPA-E project descriptions.
