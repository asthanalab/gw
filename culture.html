Group culture

Culture

We care about rigorous science, generous collaboration, and building a lab where people can grow as researchers and as colleagues.

The Asthana Group aims to combine ambitious theoretical work with an open, thoughtful, and supportive lab environment. We value curiosity, initiative, and the patience needed to learn difficult ideas well.

Group values

Mistakes

Students have the right to make mistakes. We want people to try bold ideas, learn from setbacks, and keep moving toward deeper understanding.

Multi-disciplinary collaboration

Excellent research knows no boundaries. We collaborate across chemistry, physics, materials science, computing, and applied mathematics whenever the problem demands it.

Inclusion and community

This lab is for all to experience the joy of research. No matter who you are or what your background is, we are here to support your growth as a scientist.
